Who I am

Who am I you ask? I am the owner and founder of this company, Carter Lewis. I have been doing landscape for about 2 years now and it is my dream to continue doing landscape for the rest of my days. I am thankful to have my own company for the past 2 years. I am 27 years old and currently live in my home town and state Martinsburg, West Virginia. I have lived in Martinsburg all of my life and plan to live here even longer. The heart and soul of this company has started here and I plan to end it here. I have graduated from WVU with a masters in business. I have mowed grass almost all my life, and it is one of the best things to do and a great stress releiver. Theres nothing like mowing grass all day and doing what you love all day long.
